# Akshara-Deepa Tutor

An offline-first Android study companion for 10th-grade (SSLC) students, built to bring
structured self-study tracking to students without access to guided after-school support.

## Problem
Rural students often have textbooks but no way to track chapter mastery or spot weak
subjects until final exams — by which point the learning gap is hard to close.

## Features
- **Syllabus Tracker** — checklist of chapters across Science, Math, and Social Studies
- **Quiz Mode** — 5-question timed quizzes per chapter with an answer review screen
- **Strength Map** — radar chart showing subject-wise mastery, updated after every quiz
- **Gap Area Detection** — automatically flags chapters scoring below a mastery threshold
- **Daily Reminders** — local notifications nudging students to complete one topic a day
- **100% Offline** — fully functional after the syllabus is loaded once

## Tech Stack
Kotlin · Android Studio

## Impact
Built for the "Android App Development using GenAI" internship track, aimed at improving
self-study quality and reducing learning gaps that contribute to poor exam outcomes.
